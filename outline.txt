Chapter 1 
- We meet Nick , Tom , Daisy, and Jordan
Her name was Claire Buchanan, the daughter of Daisy and Tom. A Yale law graduate, sharp and stubborn, she was everything Daisy wasn’t — independent, calculating, unafraid.

Gatsby first saw her in a Times article about digital privacy. The resemblance — the smile, the eyes — shook him. And just like that, Gatsby was pulled back into the whirlpool of obsession.
